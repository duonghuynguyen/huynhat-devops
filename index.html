    Information

    Our artisanal dough is the result of a job that has lasted a year, more than ten different flours have been tested to achieve the result we offer to our customers. It is composed of a mixture of the best Swiss IP flours, a Portuguese extra virgin organic olive oil, the best Italian yeast and Saline de Bex salt. The kneading is controlled and the dough rests for 48 hours to mature and ferment. We finalize it by putting all our passion into stretching it to shape your favorite pizza.
